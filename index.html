About

Rubab is a contemporary Pakistani designer and artist who seamlessly blends traditional craftsmanship with modern aesthetics. Her work explores the intersection of heritage and innovation, creating pieces that speak to both history and the future.

With a background in both fashion design and fine arts, Rubab's collections are characterized by their thoughtful approach to form, texture, and cultural narrative. Each piece tells a story of evolution and identity.

Her artistic practice extends beyond fashion into visual arts, where she continues to explore themes of cultural identity, abstraction, and the modern Pakistani experience.
